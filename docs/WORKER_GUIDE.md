## 1. Getting Started — Sign Up & Log In

1. **Download and open** the Service Booking app on your Android or iOS device.
2. On the **Login screen**, enter your **10-digit mobile number**.
3. Make sure the **"Worker"** role pill is selected (highlighted in green).
4. Tap **Send OTP** and enter the 6-digit code you receive.
5. Tap **Continue** to log in. Your worker account and profile are created automatically.

> 💡 **Tip:** Your phone number is your permanent account ID — no password required.  
> Demo credentials: phone `9000000002`, OTP `654321` (role: Worker).

## 3. Completing Your Profile (Onboarding)

When you first log in, a **4-step onboarding wizard** launches automatically:

### Step 1 — Basic Info
- Enter your **display name** (shown to customers).
- Add a **profile photo** (clear, professional).
- Write a short **bio** (e.g. "5 years experience in residential cleaning").

### Step 2 — Skills
- Select **all services** you can provide from the grid:  
  Cleaning · Plumbing · Electrical · Carpentry · Painting · Pest Control
- You can add more skills later from the Settings screen.

### Step 3 — Documents
- Upload a **government-issued ID** (Aadhaar card, driving licence, or passport).
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF — max **5 MB**.
- The image must be clear and fully readable.

### Step 4 — Complete
- Review a summary of your submitted profile.
- Tap **"Complete Setup"** to submit for admin review.

> ⚠️ You will **not** appear in customer searches until your profile is approved.

## 4. Verification Process

After onboarding submission:

| Stage | Description | Typical time |
|---|---|---|
| **Pending** | Profile received, under review | — |
| **Approved** ✓ | ID verified, visible to customers | Within 24 hours |
| **Rejected** | Issues found; feedback provided | Within 24 hours |

**If Rejected:**
1. Read the rejection reason shown on your Profile screen.
2. Re-upload a clearer document or correct the info.
3. Resubmit — there is no limit on resubmissions.

**Verified badge benefits:**
- ✓ badge shown on your public profile
- Priority placement in customer search results
- Access to premium service categories

## 5. Going Online & Receiving Jobs

1. Open the **Dashboard** (📋 tab).
2. Tap the **Online/Offline toggle** at the top of the screen.
   - 🟢 **Online** — you are visible to customers and receive job notifications.
   - 🔴 **Offline** — you are hidden; no new jobs will be sent to you.
3. When a customer books a service near you, your phone receives a **push notification**.
4. Open the Dashboard to see the job card.

> 💡 **Tip:** Keep your phone notifications enabled and the toggle **Online** to maximise job opportunities.

## 6. Accepting or Declining a Job

Each job card on the Dashboard shows:
- **Service name** and package details
- **Customer name** and neighbourhood
- **Scheduled date and time**
- **Distance** from your current location
- **Status badge** (Pending / Accepted / Rejected / Missed)

**To accept:**
1. Tap **"Accept"** on the job card.
2. The booking is locked to you — other workers are notified it's taken.
3. The status changes to **Accepted**.

**To decline:**
1. Tap **"Reject"** to pass on the job.
2. The status changes to **Rejected** and the customer is notified.

> ⚠️ If you neither accept nor decline within the notification window, the job is marked **Missed** and sent to the next available worker.

---

## 7. Completing the Job

1. Navigate to the customer's address shown in the booking details.
2. If needed, use **in-app chat** (available after accepting) to confirm access instructions.
3. Perform the service professionally.
4. Collect payment from the customer if the payment method is **Cash**.
5. The booking transitions to **Completed** once the customer confirms, or automatically after the scheduled end time passes.

> 💡 **Tip:** Arrive on time and communicate proactively. This directly improves your rating.

---

## 8. Chatting with Customers

1. Find an **Accepted** job on the Dashboard.
2. Tap **"Open Customer Chat"** on the job card.
3. Type a message and press Send.
4. Chat is available from the moment you accept until the booking is completed.

**Best practices:**
- Confirm arrival time 30 minutes before the job.
- Notify the customer immediately if you are running late.
- Ask about specific requirements or access restrictions.

## 9. Tracking Your Earnings

Open the **Earnings tab** (💰) from the bottom bar:

### Summary Cards (top row)

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Earned** | Sum of all gross booking amounts |
| **Net Payout** | Your earnings after 15 % platform commission |
| **Pending** | Number of payouts not yet transferred |

### Transaction History

Each row shows:
- Booking ID
- Gross amount (full booking price)
- Commission percentage applied
- **Net amount** — what you take home
- Payout status: **Paid** (green) or **Pending** (amber)

**Payout schedule:**
- Payouts are processed every **Monday** for the previous week's completed bookings.
- Funds are transferred to your registered bank account within 2 business days.

**To add your bank account:**
1. Go to Profile → Settings → Bank Account.
2. Enter IFSC code, account number, and account holder name.
3. A ₹1 test deposit verifies the account.

## 10. Managing Your Availability

Keep your calendar updated so customers only see accurate slots:

1. Go to **Profile → Availability**.
2. Set your **weekly recurring schedule** (e.g. Mon–Sat 09:00–18:00).
3. To block a specific date (vacation, appointment):
   - Tap **"Block a Date"**.
   - Select the date and optionally a time range.
4. Save changes.

> 💡 **Tip:** Up-to-date availability prevents double bookings and reduces last-minute cancellations.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I change my service skills after onboarding?**  
A: Go to Profile → Skills and update your list. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: When exactly do I get paid?**  
A: Every Monday for bookings completed the previous week (Monday–Sunday).

**Q: What is the 15% platform commission?**  
A: We deduct 15% from each booking to cover payment processing, insurance, and platform costs. Your net earning is shown transparently on every transaction.

**Q: What if a customer cancels after I've accepted?**  
A: You receive a **partial cancellation fee** (typically 30% of the booking price) if the customer cancels after you've accepted and within 2 hours of the job start time.

**Q: Can I set a minimum job value?**  
A: Not currently. This feature is on our roadmap.

**Q: What if a customer is rude or unsafe?**  
A: You can end the job safely and raise a **Dispute** from the booking detail screen. Our team will review within 24 hours.

**Q: How do I raise a dispute?**  
A: Open the booking card → scroll to the bottom → tap **"Raise a Dispute"** → select a reason and describe the issue.

**Q: Can I work in multiple cities?**  
A: Yes. Update your service area in Profile → Settings → Service Area. Customers within that radius will see you.
